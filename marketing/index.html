About Remix

Built for complexity. Driven by data.

Remix Properties LLC is a New York–based strategic advisory practice founded by Stefan Martinovic. We partner with investors, developers, and operators to source, underwrite, and execute commercial real estate opportunities across the United States — powered by institutional-grade analytics and deep market knowledge.

Our edge is integration: combining traditional deal execution with location intelligence, AI-driven analysis, and technology strategy to unlock value that conventional approaches miss.

Principal

Stefan Martinovic

A career built at the intersection of real estate and technology. Stefan has led acquisitions and asset management across a 1,500+ unit national portfolio, and served as VP at Placer.ai, a $1.5B location analytics platform. He launched NYC's MLS as the first Director of Innovation at REBNY, co-founded a VC-backed geospatial data startup, and has deployed ML models to optimize luxury high-rise developments. His work spans institutional portfolio strategy, PropTech venture investing, AI-driven underwriting, and public-sector grant capture. He teaches CRE Entrepreneurship at NYU Schack and is a member of ULI's Technology and Innovation Council. Stefan holds an MBA in Finance from The College of William & Mary and a BBA in Finance from Seton Hall University.
